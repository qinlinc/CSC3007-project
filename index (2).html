Energy Production in Different State at 2020

With the leaflet map, we can easily identify the energy produced by different state as compared to each other. The darker area indicate more energy is produced. Areas with high energy production is an indication of high natural resources available in the region.

Data

Geograpgic geometry data in form of polygons to mark out the different state of USA

Task

The visualisation shows the energy produced in different states of USA

Idiom

The visualisation is a leaflet map that helps to display the amount of energy produced in the different state through the usage of color hue. Darker color represents more energy is produced.
