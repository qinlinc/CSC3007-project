    Keep everything simple

    Our goal of our visualisation is to ensure that everything is simple and understand. We ensure that unneccessary data will not be included in our visualisation.

    There are alot of different kind of data available to use for US energy but their units are different. There are also missing data which make it hard to make fair comparison. To solve the issue, we explore each of the dataset and find out if dataset suit our use-case
