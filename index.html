Total energy produced each year

The graph shows an overview trend of the total energy produced in USA over the years. The energy produced can be a reliable way to find out about the country economy and well-being. From the graph, we can see that the energy production is stably increasing from 1960 to 2019 but it decreases from 2019 to 2020. The decrease of the energy production from 2019 to 2020 is due to the covid panademic.

    Design Principle

    Keep everything simple

    Our goal of our visualisation is to ensure that everything is simple and understand. We ensure that unneccessary data will not be included in our visualisation.

    Include interactivity in our graph

    Our graphs are interactivity by adding tooltip to allow user to view the information in detail

    Pitfalls

    There are alot of different kind of data available to use for USA energy but their metric units are different. There are also missing data which make it hard to make fair comparison as conversion varies based on the type of metric used and the type of energy source. To solve the issue, we explore each of the dataset and extract dataset available for us and suited our use-case

    Future Enhancement

    The line chart can be enhanced by adding the total energy consumption to compare with the total energy production for each year so that we can visualise if the total energy produced is enough for the US residents to consume.

    The map can display different type of energy and switch between consumption and production

    The double bar chart can be sorted based on consumption or production
