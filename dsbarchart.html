US Total Production and Consumption by State

Year 2020

The graph shows the total energy produced and consumed in US by State. From the visualization, it can be seen that Texas produced the most and also consumed the most energy. As it leads the nation in energy consumption across all sectors, it is the largest energy-consuming state in the nation. For states that have produced more energy than energy consumed, it shows that there are more power plants and lesser residential housing.
